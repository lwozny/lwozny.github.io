I am a Full Professor at the SGH Warsaw School of Economics, Department of Quantitative Economics. 

My research interests include behavioral aspects of decision making including time consistency, self control, uncertainty aversion and altruism. I am also developing some results in stochastic and large games. Tools I have extended include monotone comparative statics and its dynamic counterpart as well as a methodology of analyzing recursive competitive equilibria.
